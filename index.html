Christopher R. Karsten

Christopher R. Karsten is a trusted attorney with a practice focused on residential and commercial real estate transactions, trust and estate planning, and business and litigation matters. He represents individuals, families, and small businesses throughout the Chicago area with professionalism, responsiveness, and practical insight.

Chris regularly handles home purchases and sales, commercial property transactions, will and trust formation, estate administration, and business structuring. He also draws on his background as a trial attorney to assist clients in select litigation and dispute resolution matters. Whether helping a family plan for the future or guiding a business through a complex property deal, he provides thoughtful, personalized legal support at every step.
